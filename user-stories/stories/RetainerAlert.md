# RetainerAlert

RetainerAlert tracks freelance retainer hours with visual progress, auto-alerts, and one-click invoices to prevent overwork and revenue loss.[web:109]

## Product Vision
Freelancers burn out tracking hours manually; simple dashboard prevents disputes.[web:110]

## Target Users
Solo freelancers on monthly retainers with 3-10 clients.

## Why It Exists
9% MRR lost to billing errors; no simple tool for irregular income.[web:109]

## Core Features
- Add client/retainer hours limit
- Log time entries with timer
- Dashboard % bar, alerts at 80%/100%

## Requirements
User logs in, adds clients/limits. Starts timer or manual entry. Sees progress. Gets email alert. Clicks 'Invoice' to PDF summary.

## Constraints
Max 10 clients. No teams/integrations. Manual time only.

## Success Criteria
50% weekly active. $5 ARPU. 90% retention month 1.

Monetization: $5/mo unlimited clients.
